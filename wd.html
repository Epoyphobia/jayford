Witch Doctor















Paralyzing Cask

Launches a cask of paralyzing powder that ricochets between enemy units, stunning and damaging those it hits.
Each bounce deals more damage than the previous one. Deals 150% damage to creeps.

Voodoo Restoration

Witch Doctor focuses his magic to heal nearby allied units, costing Witch Doctor mana every second
Voodoo Restoration is active.

Maledict

Curses all enemy Heroes in a small area, causing them to take a set amount of damage each second,
as well as bursts of damage every 4 seconds based on how much health
they have lost since the curse began.

Death Ward

CHANNELED - Summons a deadly ward to attack enemy heroes within its attack range.
Lasts a maximum of 8 seconds. Death Ward has 50% bonus accuracy.
