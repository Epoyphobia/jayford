Vengeful Spirit















Magic Missile

Fires a magic missile at an enemy unit, stunning and dealing damage.

Wave Of Terror

Vengeful Spirit lets loose a wicked cry, weakening the total attack
damage and armor of enemies, and giving vision of the path ahead.

Vengeance Aura

Vengeful Spirit's presence increases the damage of nearby friendly heroes.

Nether Swap

Instantaneously swaps positions with a target Hero, friend or enemy.
Nether Swap interrupts channeling abilities on the target.
Enemy Swapped units take damage. Vengeful Spirit and allied swapped units gain a barrier equal to the damage dealt.
