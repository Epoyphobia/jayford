About Us

MISSION

Provide a Deep and Challenging Gaming Experience: Dota 2 aims to offer a complex and strategic gaming experience.
The game is designed to challenge players with its deep mechanics, requiring teamwork, skill, and strategic thinking to succeed.

Foster a Thriving Esports Community: Dota 2 has a significant presence in the esports landscape, with flagship tournaments like The International drawing global attention.
The mission includes supporting a competitive environment where professional players can showcase their talents and compete for substantial prize pools.

Community Engagement and Contribution: Valve encourages community involvement and contributions.
This is seen through the Steam Workshop, where players can create and submit cosmetic items, and events like the "Suggest a Hero" contest, where the community can participate in the hero design process.

Maintain a Balanced and Diverse Hero Roster: Dota 2 is committed to providing a balanced and diverse set of heroes.
Regular updates and patches aim to address balance issues, introduce new heroes, and ensure that the game remains dynamic with various viable strategies.

Embrace Global Diversity: Dota 2 has a massive global player base, and the game is designed to be accessible and enjoyable for players from different regions and cultures.
The international appeal contributes to the game's popularity.

Continuous Development and Improvement: Valve is committed to the ongoing development and improvement of Dota 2.
Regular patches, updates, and the addition of new content demonstrate the dedication to keeping the game fresh, balanced, and engaging for players.

VISION

Complex and Strategic Gameplay: Dota 2 is known for its deep and complex gameplay.
The game encourages strategic thinking, teamwork, and skillful execution of abilities.
The depth of its mechanics provides players with a continuously evolving learning experience.

Balanced and Diverse Heroes: One of Dota 2's strengths is its roster of heroes, each with unique abilities and playstyles.
The game aims to maintain balance among these heroes, creating an environment where diverse strategies can succeed.

Community-Driven Development: Dota 2 has a strong connection with its player community.
Valve embraces community contributions through items, cosmetics, and even hero design contests.
The community's feedback plays a role in shaping the game's direction.

Competitive Esports Scene:Competitive Esports Scene: Dota 2 has a thriving esports scene, with major tournaments like The International drawing significant attention.
The game's vision includes supporting a competitive environment for players and teams to showcase their skills on a global stage.

Continuous Updates and Improvements: Valve consistently releases updates to Dota 2 to balance heroes, introduce new content, and address community concerns.
The commitment to keeping the game fresh and engaging is evident in the regular updates and patches.

Global Player Community: Dota 2's vision includes fostering a global player community.
The game is played by millions of players worldwide, and its international appeal contributes to its success.
