Earthshaker















Fissure

Slams the ground with a mighty totem, creating an impassable ridge of stone while stunning
and damaging enemy units along its line.

Enchant Totem

Empowers Earthshaker's totem, causing it to deal extra damage and have 100 bonus attack
range on the next attack.

Aftershock

Causes the earth to shake underfoot, adding additional damage and stuns to nearby enemy units
when Earthshaker casts his abilities.

Echo Slam

Shockwaves travel through the ground, damaging enemy units.
Each enemy hit causes an echo to damage nearby units. Real heroes cause two echoes.
